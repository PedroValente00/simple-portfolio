About us

Welcome to Woof. We are a company that loves dogs. Those fluffly beautiful balls of love. We at Woof believe dogs, these angelical beings, deserve only the best and we are here to provide love and affection to them. Do you have a busy life and find yourself not having enough time to pamper your pup as much as he or she deserves it? Look no farther for this is why we are here.

Woof will care for your pupper like he or she truly deserves. Leave your 4-legged girls and boys with us for a day of activities or relaxation. We at Woof guarantee satisfaction - just don't blame us if your doggo "woofs" at you when you come to pick it up.

Services

Walks

We at Woof will walk your dog like no tomorrow.

Petting

We at Woof truly believe dogs deserve only the best. We'll pet the heck out of your doggo.

Treats

Woof will give your doggy lots of treats (and cuddles). We have a selection of both tasty and nutritional treats for your beautiful animal.

Beauty treatments

At Woof we care about the mental health of your best friend. Leave him or her with us and we'll bathe the majestic beast, comb their hair and stroke their supple bodies. We guarantee a fully relaxed feline or canine when you pick it up.
